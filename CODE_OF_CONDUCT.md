# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically rigorous
community. Be considerate, assume good intent while evaluating evidence, give
actionable feedback, and respect differing experience and perspectives.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sustained
disruption, unwanted sexual attention, publishing another person's private
information, or other conduct that a reasonable person would consider
inappropriate in a professional community.

## Scope

This standard applies in project spaces and when representing the project in
public spaces. It applies to maintainers, contributors, and participants.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through GitHub's reporting or moderation
features for the relevant repository or organization. Do not include secrets,
credentials, or production data. Maintainers may remove content, restrict
participation, or take other proportionate action after reviewing available
context. Enforcement decisions remain with authorized humans and will respect
privacy as far as practical.
